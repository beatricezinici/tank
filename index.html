Bye Bye Acrylic


The product

Our product is an acrylic nail remover and why this product will help is when you take off acrylic nails normally it hurts and this would be a quick easy way to do just that!

Mission Statment

Our mission here is to create the most cost effecient and painless way to remove those stubborn acylic nails both men and woman struggle with. All to help people be inspired with the oppurtunity of creating and getting new nails to express themselves without the pain of hurting your nails or wallets!

How it works

How it works is you pour a solution into a bowl the product comes with and you soak your nails in it for 5 minutes and they come off, Just like that!
